YaLLa 2018 Recap

30/12/2018

Looking back at 2018 it proved to be a big year for us as we went on to welcoming new teams and partners to our YaLLa family, achieving victory in various tournaments and hitting massive milestones throughout the year. For us to pursue and reach our goals that we had set, we explored new grounds and expanded in various areas. This resulted in achieving the following:

  • Mazen’s victory at WESG Hearthstone tournament in China
  • Welcoming a Stream team to the YaLLa family
  • Signing of a Rainbow Six: Siege team
  • Welcoming Yash and Wass to the YaLLa family as content creators
  • Entrance into the Fortnite scene
  • 2nd Place in R6 tournament at GamersCon Jeddah
  • Asgard as YaLLa Game Hub
  • Logitech partnership
  • Return to Overwatch

These milestones were only a scratch of the 365 days that shaped our year all thanks to our fans, players and partners. Make sure to follow us on our social media to stay connected and be part of the beginning of our 2019 YaLLa adventure.
