YaLLa Esports and Logitech at Games Con

29/10/2018

Logitech participated at this year’s Games Con in Abu Dhabi, showcasing its latest portfolio of Logitech G products and also teamed up with one of the leading eSports teams, YaLLa Esports, offering expert advice in computer gaming to visitors at this year’s event.

The gaming sub-teams, including the Rainbow Six Seige Team, Stream Team, Call of Duty Team and Hearthstone Team, of the regionally famed YaLLa Esports team held coaching clinics throughout the three-day event, sharing gaming tips and tricks with visitors at Logitech’s stand. Each team gave first-hand demonstrations on how to best play their speciality games and showcased the teams passion in action.

On display at Games Con, were the latest products from Logitech G including G305 LIGHTSPEED Wireless Gaming Mouse, G PRO Wireless Gaming Mouse, G613 Wireless Keyboard and G Pro Headset as well as the Astro headset line-up, designed with gamers in mind.

“In a young market like the UAE, where there is a large population of gamers, events like Games Con provide us with a platform to showcase our latest gaming products and a chance for visitors to experience the products first-hand. The coaching clinic with the YaLLa Esports team was a big hit amongst gaming fans and attendees, both for the experts in the computer gaming world and for the aspiring newbies. With the introduction of these new products, we will be able to offer a new and enhanced gaming experience to consumers in this region.” Andy Hoekstra, Head of Logitech MENA
